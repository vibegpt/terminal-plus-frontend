SINGAPORE CHANGI AIRPORT (SIN) - COMPLETE AMENITIES LIST
========================================================

This file contains all amenities from Singapore Changi Airport across all terminals (T1, T2, T3, T4, and Jewel), with their associated vibe tags.

VIBE TAGS LEGEND:
- Shop: Retail stores, shopping, duty-free
- Refuel: Food & dining establishments  
- Quick: Fast service options
- Explore: Attractions, art installations, gardens
- Chill: Relaxation areas, lounges
- Comfort: Wellness, spa services
- Connect: Business services, WiFi, charging
- Work: Business centers, meeting rooms
